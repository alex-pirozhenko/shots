Tahoe twilight

Vibrant colors over a beautiful lake

During our last trip to Tahoe I had an opportunity to take a few shots during the twilight hours.

In the morning I woke up well before the sunrise and headed off to the shore. My reward for an early rise was an empty beach and vibrant colors in the sky.

Minutes before the sunrise (view in full size)

After a few minutes of searching for the perfect spot, I finally noticed a thick layer of fog covering the lake.

Rising sun shines on a foggy lake (view in full size)

Shortly after, I was blessed with the first rays of sun rising above the nearby mountains.
